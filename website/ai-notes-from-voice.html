AI Notes Workflow

Use your voice to create notes you can actually use.

AI notes from voice are not just transcripts. The useful version is a workflow: speak freely, preserve the raw thought, then shape it into a summary, decisions, and next steps. That is the direction VoltType is built around.

Capture the rough thought

Talk through the meeting, brainstorm, or follow-up while the idea is fresh.

Run an AI command

Ask for a summary, bullet points, action items, or a more formal rewrite.

Save the usable version

Keep the cleaned note, share it, or turn it into the next message you need to send.

Good use cases

  • Meeting recaps right after a call
  • Founder notes after a sales conversation
  • Personal planning and quick daily reviews
  • Turning a spoken draft into a team update
  • Capturing technical context before it gets lost

Why this matters

People usually speak faster than they type, but raw transcripts are often messy. The real value comes from getting both speed and structure: fast capture first, then AI cleanup that turns the speech into something worth keeping.
